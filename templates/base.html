Big Cats of Planet Earth

Are you a fan of Lions, Tigers and other Big Cats? Then this is the site for you.

There are 7 main Big Cats around ther world, they are found on the continents of Africa, Asia and America.

Big Cats are 1 of the 2 great tribes of carnivores, the other being Wild Dogs. They are all apex predators in their range.

This site will allow ou to find out information on Lions, Tigers, Cheetahs, Leopards, Jaguars, Snow Leopards and Pumas. Find out about each cat's charactersitics, habitat, diet, competing with other predators and their relationship with the big cat's greatest enemy: Humans.

You will also find information on some big cats that lived in prehistoric times long before both the present day and after the extinction of the Dinosaurs.
