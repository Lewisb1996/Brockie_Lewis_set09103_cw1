Big Cats of Asia

Asia is home to the most subspecies of big cat species and they are found in amost every part.

The Tiger (Panhera Tigris)

The Tiger is the largest of the big cats and there are 9 subspecies including the Bengal (most well known), Siberian (biggest) and Sumatran (smallest).

Main Characteristics: They have a body length of between 4.5 - 9.25 feet long and can weigh up to 300 kgs depending on which species. All Tigers are an orange/brown colour with black stripes, but there are also rare White Tigers. Unlike most cats, the Tiger loves the water and they ar every good swimmers.

Habitat: Tigers inhabit a ride range of habitats including woodland forests, rainforests and snowy mountains.

Diet: Tigers are apex predators wherever they are and feed primary on Deer, and Wild Boar. They will also take Water Buffalo, Gaur, Bears, Crocodiles and even young Rhinos and Elephants.

Rivals and Predators: Tigers have competition with Dholes, Wolves, Bears and Crocodiles, which are all capable of killing Tigers. Humans are their main predators due to poaching and also because some Tigers will actively prey on Humans and they have caused some Tigers to become extinct

The Leopard (Panthera Pardus)

There are a total of 7 Leopard subspecies living in Asia including the Indian, Sri Lankan and Amur.

Main Characteristics: They have a body length of 3.5 - 5 feet long and weigh up to 72 kgs, dpedning on which species. Like African Leopards, these ones are all great climbers and accomplisheds stalkers, even raiding human towns for food. Some Leopards can be born black and are known to people as "Black Panthers".

Habitat: Asian leopards inhabit jungles, woodland forests, mountains desrts and even around human towns.

Diet: Asian Leopards will prey on almost anthing including Deer, Antelope, Monkeys, Wild Boars, Buffalo calves and even Bear cubs and small Crocodiles.

Rivals and Predators: Leopards face competiton with almost all other predators in Asia including Tigers, Wild Dogs, Bears and Crocodiles. Due to them preying livestock, they are killed by Humans, which have caused some Leopards like the Amur Leopard to become Critically Endangered.

The white camouflages big cat that is sometimes almost impossible to see.
